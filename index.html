What is "Green" People?

"Green vibe" people are those who embody a lifestyle deeply connected to sustainability, nature, and environmental consciousness. They are individuals who prioritize eco-friendly choices in their daily lives, demonstrating a strong commitment to reducing their environmental impact. This group of people is characterized by their deep respect for nature, a passion for preserving the earth, and a dedication to living in ways that benefit both the planet and society.
